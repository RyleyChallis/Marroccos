MARROCCO'S

The Greatest Italian in All of East Sussex

Four Generations of Italian Passion

Since Renato and Maria Marrocco opened our doors on the Hove seafront in 1969, we've been more than just a restaurant—we're a family tradition.

  • From Renato's 4 AM secret gelato recipes to our signature seafood platters and hand-rolled pasta, every dish is a piece of the "old country" served right by the sea.

  • Whether you're joining the legendary summer queue for a pistachio cone or settling into a booth for fresh Lobster Linguine, welcome to the family.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got a question? Check here first—you might find the answer instantly!

Prenotazioni / Do you take reservations?

We primarily operate on a walk-in basis to keep things fair for everyone enjoying the sunny terrace.

Dieta / Do you offer gluten-free or vegan options?

Absolutely. We offer gluten-free pasta substitutes and several vegan-friendly gelato options.

Asporto / Can I order to go?

Yes, we do! You can order our full range of gelato and most of our main menu for takeaway.

Il Dehor / Do you have outdoor seating?

We have a beautiful outdoor terrace overlooking the Kings Esplanade. It is the perfect spot for an espresso in the morning sun or a glass of wine as the sun sets over Hove.

Bambini e Cani / Are children and dog welcome?

We are a family-run business, so children are always welcome with a smile. Well-behaved dogs are also permitted in our outdoor seating area—we might even have a treat waiting for them!
